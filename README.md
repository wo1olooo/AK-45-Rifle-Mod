# AK-45 — The Perfected Prototype

---

While the AK-44 was a fractured dream of the past, the **AK-45** represents the moment that dream finally held together. Recovered from the deep-storage vaults of a forgotten Siberian research bunker, this weapon is the missing link in the evolution of the assault rifle.

Following the structural failures of the earlier AK-44, the visionary Kuzmichev returned to the drawing board in 1945. He reinforced the receiver, refined the gas system, and finally tamed the violent energy of the **7.62×41mm** cartridge. On the Rim, where every shot counts, this rifle is no longer a "failed experiment"—it is a reliable dealer of absolute stopping power.

Heavier than the AK-47 that would eventually succeed it, the AK-45 is a weapon for those who value impact over weight. It is the bridge between experimental ambition and battlefield reality.

---

⚙ **Features**

* **The Missing Link:** Adds the AK-45, the refined successor to the AK-44 prototype.
* **7.62×41mm Power:** Utilizes the potent intermediate cartridge, offering a significant damage boost over standard 7.62x39mm rounds.
* **Reliable Performance:** Unlike its predecessor, the AK-45 features improved stability and reduced jam chance (simulated through accuracy stats).
* **Hard-Hitting:** High stopping power capable of staggering mid-sized mechanoids and armored raiders.
* **Late-Tier Industrial:** Integrated into the Soviet Rifle Research tree as a high-end alternative to standard assault rifles.
